Who are we?

IoT Geeks is a non-profit hobbyist technology community in Chennai. We at IoT Geeks constantly educating people about emerging technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality(AR), 3D-Printing, Data Analytics, Machine Learning and Autonomous.
